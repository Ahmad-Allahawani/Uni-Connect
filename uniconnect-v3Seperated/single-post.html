How does AVL tree rotation actually work?

I understand BST insertion but I'm confused about when AVL trees perform rotations for the 4 cases: LL, LR, RR, RL. When does each happen and why do some need double rotation?

AVL rotations restore the height balance property. Here are all 4 cases:

🔄 LL Case — Single Right Rotation. Imbalance in left subtree's left child.

🔄 RR Case — Single Left Rotation. Mirror of LL.

🔄🔄 LR Case — Double Rotation. First left rotate on left child, then right rotate on original.

🔄🔄 RL Case — Double Rotation. Mirror of LR.

// LR Case: Step 1: Left rotate on left child Step 2: Right rotate on original node
